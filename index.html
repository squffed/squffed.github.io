FEATURED WORK - ARIA DISCONNECT

Role: Co-Developer

Aria Disconnect is a 16-bit action-adventure. You are the protagonist Heri and you are going to follow the journey of his life crisis. The year is 2082, you are hooked to a machine, and there is only one way out - to disconnect.

Kimmo Savilampi is the main developer behind all of this. I've been around to invent back-end systems like the ring menu, but more notably large scale projects like a water physics system and a live midi player. This was happening within GameMaker Studio 2 before the decision to port to Unity was made.

Now that we're on track to success, deciding the game is for Unity was a great decision. So many things are easier, and so we can get the game out faster for you folks with fancier water and fantastic gameplay!
